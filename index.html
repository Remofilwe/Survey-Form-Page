University Course Survey Form 2020

The purpose of the course feedback system is to systematically collect feedback from all credit-earning education for degree students at the University. The feedback is used in the development of teaching (teachers, departments, faculties and independent institutes), the strategic planning of education and research purposes. Primarily, the purpose of feedback is to help teachers to develop their teaching. The feedback is collected through an electronic system of the University. Teachers have an obligation to provide counter feedback to students.
